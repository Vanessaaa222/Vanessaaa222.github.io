Industry Pain Points: The "Chokepoint" in Robotics—The Heart of the Machine

The reduction gear is the highest-cost core component in industrial robots, accounting for the largest proportion of total expenses. As the era of humanoid robots approaches its explosive growth phase, the market urgently demands a revolution in power transmission technology.

Performance Gap

Future Challenges
